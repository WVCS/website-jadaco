A little about me!

Bio

I am currently a Senior at Westview High School with a prospective graduation date of 2023. In school, I am involved in Link Crew as a leader for freshmen and play soccer at the varsity level. I have been a "link leader" since my junior year and have played soccer all four years.


Outside of school, I volunteer at an organization known as TVIA, or Teen Volunteers in Action. I serve as the current Treasurer and Class Representative, and served as the Peer Mentoring Lead for the 2021-2022 year. I have played club soccer since 2014 and have served as the captain on many of these teams.


I am a PADI certified scuba diver and CPR certified, giving me essential tools to follow my hobbies as well as positively affect society. Additionally, I have a pet-sitting business to help fund scuba as well as other necessary activities.
